About this tool:

This tool does not transmit or retain the password entered

Tips for creating a strong password:

  • Use passphrases; which are easier for humans to remember but harder for computers to guess
  • Length is more important than complexity
  • Avoid easily guessed phrases related to ACME, family, pets, birthdays, etc.
  • Avoid reusing passwords or repeating words or characters
